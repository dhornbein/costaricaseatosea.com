George Hornbein
I have made two hikes across Costa Rica, one in December of 2017 with my friend Gail Haggard and one in December, 2018 with my son Drew and Rich Larson, aka Skittles. Both hikes were made with our guide, Orlando Zamares, who has crossed the country more than 30 times. Drew and I both wrote of our adventure.

Drew Hornbein
On November 30th, 2018 I set off on an adventure with my 80-year-old dad, our friend Skittles, and our guide Orlando on a hike across Costa Rica from the Caribbean coast to the Pacific. I kept a written journal and captured the random tangents of a wandering mind.
